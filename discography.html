Discography

Erik's Lament

Released: October 2022

4 years after the release of their first full-length studio album, The Blessing Way has returned with a concept album based on Gaston Leroux’s The Phantom Of The Opera.

Erik’s Lament features 11 classical inspired black metal songs which boast Gothic inspired piano work, intricate and melodic guitar riffs and and operatic vocals to compliment the theme of the album.

Mixed and Mastered at Trackmix Studio, Dublin, Ireland.



From Empty Plates We Dine

Released: February 2018

The first full-length studio album from The Blessing Way tells a chilling story of decay and the macabre, laced with the esoteric of gothic Victorian New Orleans.

Each composition features stellar orchestral songwriting reminiscent of the baroque era, harpsichords and pianos dancing over the relentless metal aspect of the music.

Mixed and Mastered at Trackmix Studio, Dublin, Ireland.
