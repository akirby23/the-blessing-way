Welcome to the official website of The Blessing Way

The Blessing Way is a solo melodic heavy metal project from Galway, Ireland.

Formed by Ollie Gill in 2008, the purpose of the band is to create dark yet melodic music influenced by the early albums of bands such as Dark Tranquility, Dissection & Cradle of Filth.
