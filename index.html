The Blessing Way is a solo melodic heavy metal project from Galway, Ireland. It was formed by Ollie Gill in 2008 with the purpose of creating dark yet melodic music influenced by the early albums of bands such as Dark Tranquility, Dissection & Cradle of Filth.
